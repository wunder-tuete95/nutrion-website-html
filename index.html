How It Works

1

Initial Assessment

We analyze your goals, training schedule, diet history, and competition plans to create your personalized nutrition strategy.

This includes:

  • Comprehensive questionnaire about your health history and goals
  • Body composition analysis using submitted photos
  • Detailed review of your current diet approach
  • Analysis of your training schedule and intensity
  • Assessment of your competition calendar and weight management needs
2

Custom Meal Plan

Within 5 business days, you'll receive your initial nutrition plan designed specifically for your body, training style, and lifestyle needs.

Your plan includes:

  • Daily meal structure tailored to your training schedule
  • Customized meal options based on your food preferences
  • Simple, easy-to-follow recipes and meal ideas
  • Timing recommendations for optimal performance and recovery
  • Personalized supplement recommendations to support your goals
3

Ongoing Support

Direct WhatsApp access to your nutritionist for questions, challenges, and adjustments as your training evolves. We're here to support you every step of the way.

Our support includes:

  • Unlimited WhatsApp messaging with quick response times
  • Advice for navigating special occasions and travel
  • Real-time troubleshooting for challenges you encounter
  • Adjustments to your plan as your training schedule changes
  • Guidance for implementing your nutrition plan in real-world situations
4

Regular Check-ins

Weekly progress tracking ensures your nutrition plan evolves with your body and goals. Based on your feedback and results, we'll make personalized adjustments to optimize your plan.

Check-ins cover:

  • Weekly progress reports submitted through a simple check-in form
  • Weight and body composition monitoring
  • Energy levels and performance assessments
  • Feedback on meal adherence and satisfaction
  • Personalized tips and recommendations based on your reported experiences
  • Precise plan adjustments based on your results and feedback
5

Video Consultations

In-depth video calls to discuss progress, make major adjustments, and plan for upcoming competitions. Depending on your selected plan, these can be weekly or monthly. We'll continuously refine your plan based on your changing needs.

Consultations include:

  • Convenient scheduling through our easy-to-use booking calendar
  • Comprehensive review of your progress since the last call
  • Detailed discussion of what's working and what's not
  • Strategic planning for upcoming training blocks
  • Education on nutrition principles relevant to your goals
  • Setting new targets and adjusting your plan accordingly
6

Competition Preparation

Specialized nutrition timing and strategies for reaching your ideal weight while optimizing performance. We'll help you peak at exactly the right time for competition day.

Preparation includes:

  • Strategic weight management approaching competition
  • Carbohydrate manipulation for optimal energy levels
  • Hydration protocols for weigh-ins and competition
  • Day-of-competition fueling strategies
  • Recovery nutrition for multi-match tournaments

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Nutrion different from other nutrition programs? +

Nutrion is specifically designed for BJJ athletes, with nutrition plans that work around your training schedule. Your coach is both a nutritionist and an active BJJ black belt who understands the unique demands of the sport from first-hand experience. This dual expertise allows for truly personalized guidance that generic nutrition programs simply can't offer.

I have a busy life and no established routine. Will this program work for me? +

Absolutely! We specifically design our nutrition plans around your existing schedule and preferences. The program adapts to your life, not the other way around, making it ideal for busy athletes. Our approach focuses on practical, sustainable strategies that fit into your lifestyle, rather than rigid meal plans that require complete lifestyle changes.

Can your program help me with weight management for competitions? +

Yes, weight management for competition is one of our specialties. Whether you're looking to cut weight, maintain, or gain muscle, our personalized approach creates sustainable plans tailored to your specific competition schedule. We provide strategies that help you reach your target weight while maintaining strength and energy for optimal performance on competition day.

What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies? +

We work with all types of dietary restrictions and preferences, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other allergy-specific needs. Your nutrition plan will be fully customized to accommodate your unique requirements while ensuring you get all the nutrients needed for optimal BJJ performance and recovery.
